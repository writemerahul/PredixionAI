Predixion AI

Voice and Chat Agent – AI Governance, Risk & Compliance Platform

Product Vision

Predixion AI is designed to become the compliance and trust layer for conversational AI in BFSI. The platform ensures voice and chat agents operate safely, ethically, and within regulatory frameworks.

---

Phase 1 – Core Platform (0–6 Months)

Goal: Launch compliant AI agents for BFSI

Conversational AI Engine

  • Voice AI calling agents
  • WhatsApp / chat AI agents
  • Payment reminder workflows
  • Human escalation support
  • Multilingual conversations

AI Guardrails Engine

  • Tone monitoring
  • Harassment detection
  • Prompt injection protection
  • Hallucination prevention
  • Conversation policy enforcement

Compliance Monitoring

  • RBI recovery rule monitoring
  • DPDP privacy compliance
  • TRAI communication compliance
  • PCI payment safety monitoring
---

Phase 2 – AI Governance Layer (6–12 Months)

Goal: Transform Predixion into AI GRC platform
  • AI risk scoring engine
  • Policy enforcement engine
  • Real-time compliance monitoring
  • AI model governance
---

Phase 3 – Trust & Safety Platform (12–18 Months)

  • Harassment detection system
  • Vulnerable customer detection
  • AI explainability engine
  • Bias and fairness monitoring
---

Phase 4 – Enterprise AI GRC (18–24 Months)

  • Regulatory mapping engine

  • Risk heatmap dashboard
  • Automated audit reporting
---

Phase 5 – AI Compliance Platform (24–36 Months)

  • Multi-bank AI governance platform
  • AI red-team testing
  • AI risk marketplace

Platform Architecture

Customer Interaction ↓ Voice / Chat AI Agents ↓ AI Guardrails Layer ↓ Compliance Monitoring Engine ↓ Risk Scoring Engine ↓ Audit Logs & Compliance Records ↓ Predixion AI Compliance Dashboard
